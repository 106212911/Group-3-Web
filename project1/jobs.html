Job Opening: Data Analyst

Reference Number: DA289

Position Title: Data Analyst

Brief Description: We are seeking a Data Analyst to collect, process, and analyze data to help drive strategic decision-making across departments.

Salary Range: $75,000 – $95,000 per annum

Reports To: Senior Data Scientist

Key Responsibilities

  • Interpret data and analyze results using statistical techniques
  • Develop and maintain dashboards and reports
  • Identify trends, patterns, and insights to support business objectives
  • Work with management to prioritize business needs
  • Clean and validate data for accuracy and completeness

Required Qualifications, Skills, and Attributes

Essential

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field
  2. 2+ years of experience in data analysis or related field
  3. Proficiency in SQL and Microsoft Excel
  4. Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Power BI, Tableau)
  5. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

Preferable

  • Knowledge of Python or R for data analysis
  • Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure)
  • Understanding of machine learning basics
